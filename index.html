Our company history

Happenings & Co. was founded in 2005 by a group of event planning enthusiasts who were passionate about creating unique and unforgettable experiences for their clients. Over the years, the company has grown to become one of the leading event planning and management firms in the region, with a reputation for excellence and a commitment to exceeding client expectations. From corporate events and product launches to weddings and private parties, Happenings & Co. has a proven track record of delivering exceptional results and creating memories that last a lifetime.
